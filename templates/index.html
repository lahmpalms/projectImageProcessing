About

Nowadays, there are many patients with chronic wounds at PSU hospital. The patient wound must be treated many times by a variety of nurses. Wound size is usually the first criterion on a wound assessment. Unfortunately, the nurse who heals the patient wound could not remember the wound size for last time heeling.

Hence, the nurse could not know the progress stage of the wound healing. To help the nursing team, monitor the size of wound for giving an appropriate wound dressing until the wound is completely healed, the author proposes a patient wound care database and a wound size monitoring by employing image processing technique.

The system will help the nursing team monitor the trend of wound size and can automatically generate many reports such as the patient wound size report.
